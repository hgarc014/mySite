Yin and Yang

The image on the top right is a small version of a Yin and Yang image that I have created (you can click the image to see the large image). The image to me represents light and dark, good and bad. No matter how good a person is they always have tiny pieces of evil inside of them. Similarly, no matter how evil a person is they always have tiny pieces of good inside of them. However, a person themselves is not mearly one side of the image. They are 2 pieces, it's just a matter of which side of the image you are looking at.
